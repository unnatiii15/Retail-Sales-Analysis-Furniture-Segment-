# 📊 Retail Sales Analysis (Furniture Segment)

## 🚀 Overview

This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover patterns in **sales performance, pricing impact, and product demand** within the furniture category.

The goal is to extract actionable insights that can support **business decisions in inventory planning and pricing strategy**.

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Records:** 2,121 transactions
* **Features:** 27
* Includes: Sales, Profit, Discount, Subcategory, Order Date, Delivery metrics

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📈 Seasonal Demand

* December recorded the highest sales (~₹121K)
* Indicates strong year-end demand surge

---

### 💸 Discount vs Profit

* Moderate negative correlation (**-0.48**) between discount and profit
* Higher discounts reduce overall profitability

---

### 🪑 Subcategory Analysis

* Furnishings (~45%) and Chairs (~29%) dominate transaction volume
* Key drivers of product demand

---

### 📊 Sales Behavior

* Sales distribution shows variability across months and product types
* Useful for demand forecasting and stock planning
